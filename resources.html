Masahiro Sakurai on Creating Games

Perhaps my favourite game dev/design resource! From the creator of Kirby and Super Smash Bros. are bite-sized but high-quality videos on advice on all aspects of game development and design!

Check it out!!

Game Maker's Toolkit

An amazing channel that analyzes various games and provides insights on game dev! Helps with developing the mindset and thinking of a game dev!

Check it out!!

Game Developers Conference

A place where you can watch all the conferences given from indie devs to the most significant and experienced in the industry. Can basically learn directly from industry professionals.

Check it out!!

The Making of TLOZ: BOTW

A resource that I personally love, as it covers the design and development process of my all-time favourite game! This is perhaps Nintendo's most public display of their design process and it's very insightful!

Check it out!!

Shigeru Miyamoto

A playlist of all Shigeru Miyamoto videos! As the creator of Zelda, Mario, and many other world-known video games, watching his interviews helps me further learn about Nintendo game dev!

Check it out!!

Satoru Iwata

A playlist of all Satoru Iwata videos! As my idol and role model, this is an incredibly important and insightful playlist for me!

Check it out!!

Iwata Asks

A collection of interviews conducted by Iwata with other employees in Nintendo on the development and design on their games! Another great insight into Nintendo's dev/design process!
