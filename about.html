Intro

Hey, my name's Rosan! I'm currently a high-school student who's super passionate about tech, programming and gaming! I love learning about and understanding the inner workings of all kinds of devices bc it feels like unlocking the secrets of "technology"! Thus you'll often find me either, taking apart a device, researching a device, creating a device, or a combination of all three in an effort to satisfy my curiosity!

My Story

Having been exposed to and grown up with technology, I feel like my interest in tech was only natural. My dad being an engineer also definitely contributed to that, as he got me into programming when I was 8. However, I feel like my own passion developed when I got my Nintendo Switch! Aside from it being the first game console and personal device I ever owned, it was also the first device that left a lasting impact on me. Maybe it was because it was just my first experience with gaming, but the versatility in gameplay with the console and how that was utilized in various games was just fascinating to me! This incited a curiosity in me where I just kept asking, "Wow, how does this work? Why and how does it make me feel this way?" Thus, my passion for tech "blossomed", in a way! I want to be able to utilize and create technology to create an impact or make a difference.
